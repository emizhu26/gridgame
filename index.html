Rules Snapshot

  • Demand each round: roll 1-16.
  • Each renewable generates 1-4 power.
  • Fossil fallback cost: ceil(fossil used / 2).
  • Income: demand - fossil cost.
  • Each renewable purchase costs 24 and replaces 1 fossil.
  • You lose instantly if demand cannot be met.
